Is Korah free to use?

Yes! Korah Beta is completely free for all users. We believe quality study tools should be accessible to everyone.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Korah stores data locally on your device. No cloud uploads, no account required, and complete offline functionality.

🍎

Currently available on iOS (TestFlight Beta) and web browsers. Android and desktop apps are coming soon.

Korah AI uses advanced language models to provide personalized explanations, generate study materials, and adapt to your learning style.

Yes! Korah includes daily streaks, task management with priority levels, and mood-based recommendations to optimize your study sessions.
